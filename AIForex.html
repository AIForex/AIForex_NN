Description

This demo, is an example of Deep Q applied to the Forex Markets, The idea is that the algorithm can help perform decision based on 3 actions, 5 inputs, with the goal of maximum pips/ points over time. Deep Q would try and avoid decisions that would produce negative reward and pick decisions to increase rewards
